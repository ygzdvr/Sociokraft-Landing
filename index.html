PALM is your ultimate shopping companion app that redefines your in-store shopping experience. It allows you to scan your receipts using the app, saving your purchased items to your profile. You can participate in surveys, earn PALM Points, and unlock exclusive discounts.

Signing up for PALM is a breeze! During the registration process, we'll ask for your phone number, name, and email address (optional). We value your privacy and ensure the security of your data.

Yes, your privacy is paramount to us. PALM employs robust data encryption and protection measures to safeguard your personal information. You can review our comprehensive privacy policy for more details.

Currently, PALM is compatible with select partnered stores, which include major supermarkets and retailers. Check the app for a list of participating locations.

Scanning your receipts is effortless. Use the PALM app's built-in scanner to capture the QR code on your receipt. Once scanned, your purchased items are instantly stored in your PALM profile for your convenience.

PALM Points are rewards you earn by participating in surveys, engaging with the app, and making purchases at partnered stores. The more you interact with PALM, the more Points you accumulate, which can be redeemed for exclusive discount coupons.
